About

Militant History reproduces historical documents (newspapers, pamphlets, books, and articles) from the Committee for a Workers’ International (CWI), from its foundation in 1974 to the formation of the International Socialist Alternative (ISA) in 2020. Our goal is to digitize and re-publish literature which is unavailable online, as well as to provide a convenient index for CWI material.

Labor Militant

Labor Militant was a semi-monthly newspaper which ran from 1986 to 1997. It shared a name with its publisher, Labor Militant, the organization that became Socialist Alternative (US) in 1999.
